    The Special Attributes

    We’ve taken this classic drink and given it a modern twist. Sure, the Goli Soda you found on the street tasted great but it was filled with questions about the water they used and how they cleaned the bottles. We’ve taken all the worry out and left you with nothing but great taste. All our bottles are UV sterilised and filled with mineral water, each flavour has a low sugar formula so you get nothing but safe, healthy and delicious Goli Soda.
